Menu

At Mia's Trattoria, our menu is a heartfelt homage to the rich and diverse culinary landscape of Italy. Crafted with passion and precision, each dish is designed to take you on an evocative journey from the sun-drenched coasts of Sicily to the rolling hills of Tuscany, reflecting the authentic flavours and time-honoured traditions that define Italian gastronomy. We believe in showcasing the true essence of Italian cooking – simple, fresh ingredients expertly combined to create dishes that are both comforting and exquisitely flavourful. Our culinary philosophy centres on celebrating the purity of ingredients and the artistry of preparation.

Begin your culinary exploration with our selection of 'Antipasti,' an array of tantalizing starters perfect for sharing and awakening your palate. Indulge in vibrant bruschetta, featuring ripe tomatoes and fragrant basil, or savour our creamy burrata paired with prosciutto di Parma. Our crispy calamari, marinated olives, and artisanal cheese boards offer a delightful prelude to your main course, each dish prepared with the freshest seasonal produce and authentic Italian components to ensure a true taste of Italy right from the start of your meal.

Dive into the heart of Italian cuisine with our 'Primi' and 'Secondi' courses. Our pasta dishes are a highlight, featuring handmade pasta crafted daily, tossed in rich, slow-simmered sauces that sing with flavour. From the classic simplicity of Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe to the robust depth of Lasagne al Forno, every bite is a testament to traditional techniques. For our main courses, explore succulent options like tender Osso Buco, grilled Branzino, or perfectly cooked chicken and veal dishes, each served with thoughtful accompaniments that complement their rich profiles.

No Italian meal is complete without the irresistible allure of pizza, and at Mia's Trattoria, our 'Pizze' are a testament to true Neapolitan tradition. Our dough is made in-house, fermented to perfection, and baked in a scorching wood-fired oven to achieve that signature blistered crust and smoky flavour. Topped with premium San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and a curated selection of gourmet ingredients, our pizzas range from the timeless Margherita to inventive combinations that delight every palate. Each slice delivers an authentic taste of Italy's most beloved street food.

Conclude your dining experience with our exquisite 'Dolci' and 'Bevande' selections. Indulge in classic desserts like creamy Tiramisu, delicate Panna Cotta, or a refreshing Lemon Ricotta Cheesecake, all crafted in-house to provide a perfect sweet ending. Complement your meal with our thoughtfully curated wine list, featuring regional Italian varietals that perfectly pair with our dishes, or choose from our selection of craft beers, traditional Italian sodas, and freshly brewed espresso and cappuccinos. We believe a good drink completes the perfect meal.
